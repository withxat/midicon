A320U.sf2 — Signal Factory Sound

Midicon uses the same default SoundFont as ryohey/signal:
https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/ryohey/signal@4569a31/public/A320U.sf2

The file is commonly known as A320U.sf2. Signal labels it "Signal Factory Sound".
Upstream packaging and license notes may appear in the signal repo and in
denemo/denemo (soundfonts/A320U-license.txt). Attribute the soundfont author when
you ship a product that bundles or redistributes the file.

GeneralUser GS is not used as the default bank in Midicon.
